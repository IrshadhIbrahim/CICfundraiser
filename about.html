About Us




Who we are

Imagine a church that would go all out to show the love of God to our generation and tell that God has a plan & purpose for their life! Imagine a church committed to feel the heart beat of Jesus Christ for the unreached especially the Asian Indians in the city of Plano & beyond! Crosspointe Indian Church has a variety of people from all over India – east, west, north & south. Having our roots in the rich and vibrant heritage of a nation that speaks 750 languages, we incorporate few of these languages during our moments of intimate worship along with contemporary worship music in English.

Whether you’re spiritually restless, discontent, new at discovering God, from a different faith or have been a veteran of the faith; You are WELCOME here. We hope & trust you will want to become a part of Crosspointe Indian Church.

Our Mission

As HIS bride the mission of Crosspointe Indian Church is to lead people to a growing relationship with Jesus Christ.

To be the Hands and Feet of Jesus Christ, to see people with His Eyes, to feel His Heartbeat for people.

Our Vision

Crosspointe Indian Church exists to passionately present Jesus Christ & make disciples of the first generation Asian Indian families, professionals and students in the greater Plano area & beyond!
